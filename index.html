关于我About Me

我是一名横跨「控制理论 × 工业智能化 × AI」的工程师。十余年里，我的工作沿着一条完整的链路推进：从控制理论的底层数学，到把控制与优化算法嵌入工业产品、让设备更智能，再到今天投身工业大模型与Agent。 I'm an engineer working across control engineering, industrial intelligence, and AI. Over the past decade my work has followed one continuous thread: from the underlying mathematics of control, to embedding control and optimization algorithms into industrial products to make equipment smarter, to today's work on industrial large models and agents.

读博期间我在 IEEE TAC、TCST 等顶刊以一作发表分布式观测器与控制器的工作；进入工业界后，我没有停在论文层面——在阿特拉斯·科普柯，作为项目负责人，把控制与优化算法嵌入到嵌入式控制器中，让产品更智能；我参与的两款产品累计销售2000余台。近两年，我主动转向AI: 从Dify二开、内部AI培训，到探索事件驱动的工业Agent架构思路。 During my PhD I published first-author work on distributed observers and controllers in IEEE TAC and TCST. In industry I didn't stop at papers — at Atlas Copco, as project lead, I embedded control and optimization algorithms into embedded controllers to make the products smarter; the two product lines I contributed to have shipped 2000+ units. In the past two years I've moved deliberately into AI: from extending Dify and running internal AI training to exploring an event-driven industrial-agent architecture.

我相信工业AI的真正壁垒，不在模型本身，而在于既懂物理系统、又懂数据与算法的人。这正是我想做的事：让大模型与Agent真正落进工业/制造业现场。 I believe the real moat in industrial AI isn't the model — it's people who understand both physical systems and data/algorithms. That's exactly what I want to do: bring large models and agents onto the real manufacturing floor.
